NUTRITION

VEGAN

A vegan diet is based on plants such as vegetables, grains, nuts and fruits and foods made from plants

LOW CARB

A low-carb diet focuses on proteins and some nonstarchy vegetables.

HIGH PROTIEN DIET

High-protein diets typically include large quantities of protein and only a small amount of carbohydrate

BALANCED DIET

Eating a wide variety of foods in the right proportions to achieve and maintain a healthy body weight.
