Fitter Health

The aim of Fitter Health is to provide a set of routine exercises. With the help of this web application, users can create or adopt new training plans. Additionally, it can be in sync with fitness trackers and provide personalized health recommendations for a user. The primary goal is to get healthy in body and mind to live a longer and more fulfilled life. It is accessible by trainers and trainees. It is of the great importance for trainers who wish to streamline workout routines.

☑️ Using AI to check the form during an exercise.
☑️ Tracks the count of exercise.
☑️ Tracks the health parameters, like BMI, Calorie Counter, of the trainee.
☑️ Providing Workout routine Suggestions, Diet Suggestions.
☑️ Provides a platform to fitness freaks to register as fitness trainers.
☑️ Takes data from the fitness band and providing it to the trainer.
